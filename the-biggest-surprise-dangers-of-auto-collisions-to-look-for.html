The Biggest Surprise Dangers of Auto Collisions to Look For.

Oct 31, 2023

There are a whole lot of unfortunate ways your vehicle can sustain damage in an accident. If left unchecked, in our experience, most of it will get worse over time. According to Broadly, the auto collision repair market is projected to reach a whopping $210 billion by 2028. That number is so large partly because of the large umbrella of work that it may entail depending on your case. If you experience a collision, ensure an auto body repair shop in your area examines the vehicle for hidden damages. Otherwise, you might end up with a severely damaged vehicle down the road. Keep an eye out for these surprising dangers of auto collisions.

Shattered Headlights

Your windows, unfortunately, may not be the only part of the car that shatters. Headlights are also particularly prone to breaking in this way on impact, making it illegal, if not impossible, to drive at night right away. If your headlight gets smashed in a collision, you'll want to take a picture immediately and then cover the affected area with clear or amber tape. It's important to make sure that no water or dirt gets into the electronics. Your local auto body repair shop can help you order a new one and get it installed as soon as possible.

Paint Damage

When your car is struck in a bad collision, your first thoughts probably turn to your own safety or whether your car is alright. If you're like many drivers, you may not notice how the collision wrecked your paint job right away. That said, damaged paint can be deleterious to the lifespan of your vehicle, and you may find yourself bothered by its appearance over time. Our team is here to mix and match the perfect color for your car to ensure it looks fresh off the line again. We have over 20 years of experience fixing up cars after collisions.
